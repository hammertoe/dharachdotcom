Hi! I'm Matt, a founder, developer and developer advocate passionate about blockchains, smart contracts, AI/ML, and Python.

As a developer, I love building things—especially finding creative ways to combine different technologies into something new and unexpected.

As a developer advocate, I enjoy helping other developers learn, experiment, and build. Let's figure things out together! I also act as the "voice of the developer"—bringing your feedback to product teams and helping shape better tools and experiences.

I'm currently living in Barbados and currently working on a number of projects:

  • Eats BB - the easiest way to find local food vendors in Barbados.
  • We Outside 246 - an AI-powered events discovery platform that automatically finds and lists all the hottest fetes, parties, concerts and limes happening in Barbados. No more missing the vibes.
  • Yuh Hear Dem - an AI guide to Barbadian Parliament that helps citizens understand what's really being said in political debates and decisions. Making democracy more accessible, one question at a time.
  • Yuh Gettin' Tru? - an AI-enhanced aggregate search across 62 stores in Barbados. Search over 283,000 products to find exactly what you need, from hardware to homewares to chocolate.
  • alpha.gov.bb - the Government of Barbados' new digital services portal. I'm helping to make government services clearer, simpler and faster for citizens to access online.
  • Untangle - a shame-free, context-aware task manager designed for ADHD brains. Lives right in WhatsApp.
